General Rules

The event is called GDSC FEST 24 and it is an international level tech fest organized by Google Developer Student Clubs of KSR Institute of Engineering and Technology. It will be a two-day extravaganza with 7+ tech events, 7+ non-tech events, 3 flagship events, hands-on workshops, and prizes worth INR 300,000. The event will be held at KSR Institute of Engineering and Technology and the dates are not mentioned in the flyer, but it does mention that you must have a Tech Visa to attend. You can buy a Tech Visa for INR 300. Here are some of the things you can do at the event:

  • Attend tech events such as hackathons, workshops, and coding competitions
  • Attend non-tech events such as quizzes, treasure hunts, and cultural events
  • Attend flagship events such as the keynote address, the panel discussion, and the award ceremony
  • Participate in hands-on workshops to learn new tech skills
  • Win prizes worth INR 300,000
